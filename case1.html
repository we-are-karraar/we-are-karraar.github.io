SolaX

SolaX Power is a clean energy solutions provider using cutting edge solar inverter technology, Solax Power has been able to produce some of the most efficient solar inverters on the market, and has a strong market presence in 66 countries via 200 distribution channels.

What did the company want to achieve

The company, with an existing call centre in Australia, wanted to onboard their European call centre on Twilio Flex. The task at hand was to exploit the call overflow across regions and route calls to underutilized call centers. Additionally, they wanted to pull in data directly from their existing CRM system

How did we help them

Karraar helped SolaX define the rules for call overflow to consider business hours across two different time zones. We simplified the call flow for the customer to handle incoming calls from different countries by developing a routing based on agent skills. The customer benefitted from our capability to develop backend and frontend hence providing a we provided a one stop solution for the client’s development requirements.

We also updated the agent UI to pull data from the CRM to appear in the correct format when an incoming call came in.
