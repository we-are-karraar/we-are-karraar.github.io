Aligntech

Align Technology (brand Invisalign) is a global medical device company that develops its own specialized software to create innovative products such as Invisalign clear aligners and OrthoCAD digital services. These help dental professionals achieve the clinical results they expect and deliver effective, cutting-edge dental options to their patients. It is a publicly traded company headquartered in San Jose.

What did the company want to achieve

The company wanted connect customers from a custom Line channel platform to their agents using Twilio Flex Contact center. The major challenge was that Line was not a native implementation supported by Twilio. Hence, Aligntech reached out to us to integrate their existing customer support channels of sms and whatsapp. They wanted to onboard new customers using a chat flow and for existing ones the chat needed to directly transfer to the agent.

How did we help them

Karraar helped them build a call flow using Twilio studio with additional error checks. The customer benefitted from the tight integrations built by us with the customer’s exiting CRM platform.
