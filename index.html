Solax Power

SolaX Power is a clean energy solutions provider using cutting edge solar inverter technology, Solax Power has been able to produce some of the most efficient solar inverters on the market, and has a strong market presence in 66 countries via 200 distribution channels.

What did the company want to achieve

The company, with an existing call centre in Australia, wanted to onboard their European call centre on Twilio Flex. The task at hand was to exploit the call overflow across regions and route calls to underutilized call centers. Additionally, they wanted to pull in data directly from their ting CRM system

How did we help them

Karraar helped SolaX define the rules for call overflow to consider business hours across two different time zones. We simplified the call flow for the customer to handle incoming calls from different countries by developing a routing based on agent skills. The customer benefitted from our capability to develop backend and frontend hence providing a we provided a one stop solution for the client&rsequo;s development requirements.

We also updated the agent UI to pull data from the CRM to appear in the correct format when an incoming call came in.

Pandemics & Borders

Pandemics & Borders is an international research group focusing on generating new data and analysis to inform complex decisions regarding the adoption and lifting of cross-border measures to control pandemics.

What did the company want to achieve

The client was seeking a way to communicate complex research about border and public health policy in an accessible, approachable manner to the general public. The major challenge was to ensure that this information was seamlessly displayed to the viewer.

How did we help them

Karraar designed the front-end user interface, and the backend database to contain media articles & research We also provided the client with consulting and impactful insights on on Art Direction and Visual Design to craft the intended brand identity of the organisation.

Aligntech

Align Technology (brand Invisalign) is a global medical device company that develops its own specialized software to create innovative products such as Invisalign clear aligners and OrthoCAD digital services. These help dental professionals achieve the clinical results they expect and deliver effective, cutting-edge dental options to their patients. It is a publicly traded company headquartered in San Jose.

What did the company want to achieve

The company wanted connect customers from a custom Line channel platform to their agents using Twilio Flex Contact center. The major challenge was that Line was not a native implementation supported by Twilio. Hence, Aligntech reached out to us to integrate their existing customer support channels of sms and whatsapp. They wanted to onboard new customers using a chat flow and for existing ones the chat needed to directly transfer to the agent.

How did we help them

Karraar helped them build a call flow using Twilio studio with additional error checks. The customer benefitted from the tight integrations built by us with the customer’s exiting CRM platform.
