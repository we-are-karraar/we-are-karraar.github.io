Borders & Pandemics

an international research group focusing on generating new data and analysis to inform complex decisions regarding the adoption and lifting of cross-border measures to control pandemics.

What did the company want to achieve

They wanted a way to communicate complex research about border and public health policy in an accessible, approachable manner to the general public.

How did we help them

Karraar designed the front-end user interface, and the backend database to hold media articles & research

We also updated the agent UI to pull data from the CRM to appear in the correct format when an incoming call came in.
